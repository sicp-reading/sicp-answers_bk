Exercise 1.36
=====================

:math:`x \mapsto \frac{log 1000}{log x}` の不動点を探索する.

========
解答
========

予測値を印字するよう書き換える
--------------------------------------

.. sourcecode:: scheme 

    (define (fixed-point f first-guess)
      (define (close-enough? v1 v2)
        (< (abs (- v1 v2)) tolerance))
      (define (try guess)
        (display guess)
        (newline)
        (let ((next (f guess)))
          (if (close-enough? guess next)
              next
              (try next))))
      (try first-guess))
